Project Overview

I designed the whole thing in CAD, which was actually one of the first bigger projects I tried when I started learning 3D modeling. The idea came from a couple of videos I watched that made me think, “Hey, I want to build something like that.” Here are the videos that inspired me: Raspberry Pi Retro Arcade using RetroPie MintyPi From there, I modeled every part—the front console, back console, the buttons, and the full assembly. This project helped me practice fitting pieces together, thinking about internal space, and making something that looks clean but is still functional. What you see on this page is the final version of the casing, but I plan on creating a full system along with a custom controller in the future.
